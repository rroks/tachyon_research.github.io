About us

About Tachyon

Tachyon Research Ltd. is a high-speed growth quantitative trading company of its own funds and mainly manages the general manager’s family and the company’s assets. The members of the company come from big nine investment banks, big four accounting firm, head brokers in China and top universities overseas. 100% of the company’s employees are having master’s degrees and above. We have 13 self-owned quantitative trading models, and issue independent research reports on major types of assets every month.

Our company’s main trading and research focus is on the secondary market, the stocks, bonds, foreign exchange, commodities and index funds, etc. Currently our company has three offices set in Hong Kong, Shenzhen and Xiamen and consists of Research department, Investment and Trading department, Strategic Development and Advisory department, department of Quantitative Information Technology, Investment Operations department and Marketing department.

Target Clients

  1. Individuals and institutional investors who are interested in a particular stock around the world.
  2. Students and parents who do not have much understanding of investment research.
  3. Professionals in the same industry who want to reduce their workload.
  4. Headhunting or headhunting agency

Major Departments

Research Department

Our research department focuses on primary and secondary markets. We research and analyze macroeconomic fundamentals, market strategies, stocks, bonds, foreign exchange and commodities for domestic and international clients through Tachyon Research’s platform.

Investment and Trading Department

Our investment and trading department focuses on financial and capital markets, electronics , new technology industry, big finance and TMT. We use self-financing funds to invest prudently to generate returns and provide advisory services on investment.

Strategic Development & Advisory Department

Our strategic development & advisory department involves corporate strategy making and internal staff growth system. We provide intern and work training services for our clients co-operating with top universities.

Department of Quantitative Information Technology

Our department of quantitative information technology focuses on building self-owned quantitative modals. We complete industry fundamental quantitative research strategy and project construction.

Investment Operations Department

Our investment operations department mainly focuses on providing backstage support for investment and trading department such as human resource support.

Marketing Department

Our marketing department is mainly responsible for advertising our company brand. We issue our everyday research result through our official media account to improve corporate visibility.
